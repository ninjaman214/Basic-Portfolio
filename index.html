About Me

My life accomplishment is to help others using Biomedical Engineering. Since then my vow has been to i, for the sake of the patriots that fight for this country and citizens of the world.
My experiences as a leader in various clubs and social activities will enable me to guide others in the creation of technology that will cure and stop many problems. My passion to help; I would like to leave a legacy that can be remembered and furthered exploited. I would like to be able to increase the field of Biomedical Engineering to the state that patients would feel as if their lives can be the same.


I am an African American male born and raised in Liberia West Africa. My family struggle and longing for my success are my motivations; although my back ground is different from most people it has instilled in me the desire to become a role model to other males. Thee health rate and economical corruption are major factors in the downfall of its social economy, so I plan to show my gratitude to my family and love ones by strengthening its health bringing it one step closer to the great country I know it can be.
